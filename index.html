Narendra Modi

Prime Minister of India

Narendra Modi has served as the 14th Prime Minister of India since 2014. He is known for economic reforms, foreign diplomacy, and various social initiatives.

Early Life

Born on 17 September 1950 in Vadnagar, Gujarat, Modi helped his father sell tea as a child. His journey from a small town to the highest office in India is often cited as inspirational.

Major Initiatives

  • Digital India
